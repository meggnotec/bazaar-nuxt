The fashion industry is at a crossroads. Consumers are more informed than ever, supply chains are under greater scrutiny, and the environmental cost of fast production cycles can no longer be ignored. At Bazaar Studio, sustainability is not a marketing label we attach to a capsule collection once a year. It is a set of decisions we make every day, from the fibers we source to the factories we partner with.

## Where We Started

When Bazaar Studio launched, we made a deliberate choice not to chase volume. We would rather produce fewer pieces at a higher standard than flood the market with disposable garments. That founding principle shaped everything that followed: our supplier relationships, our material selection, our packaging, and our approach to inventory.

We started by auditing every step of our supply chain. Where did our cotton come from? Who dyed our fabrics? What happened to our offcuts? The answers were not always comfortable, but they gave us a clear map of where to improve.

## Materials That Matter

### Natural and Responsible Fibers

We prioritize materials that carry recognized certifications. Our cotton is sourced from farms that meet organic or Better Cotton standards. Our wool comes from suppliers certified by the Responsible Wool Standard, ensuring animal welfare and land management practices meet rigorous benchmarks.

For synthetic materials, we are transitioning to recycled alternatives wherever performance requirements allow. Our outerwear linings now use recycled polyester derived from post-consumer plastic bottles, and our goal is to extend this across all synthetic components by the end of next year.

### Low-Impact Processing

Fabric dyeing and finishing are among the most resource-intensive stages of garment production. We work with dyehouses that use closed-loop water systems, which recycle up to ninety percent of the water used in processing. Our preferred dye partners also hold OEKO-TEX certification, which means every chemical used in production has been tested and verified as safe for human health and the environment.

## Our Factory Partners

We maintain long-term relationships with a small network of factories across Portugal, Turkey, and India. Each partner is selected based on working conditions, fair wages, and environmental practices. We visit every facility annually, and our production team conducts unannounced spot checks throughout the year.

Key standards we require from every partner:

- Living wages for all workers, not just minimum wage
- Safe and clean working environments
- No child labor or forced labor under any circumstances
- Regulated working hours with overtime compensation
- Freedom of association and the right to collective bargaining

## Packaging and Shipping

Our packaging has evolved significantly over the past two years. Every order now ships in recyclable mailers made from post-consumer waste. Tissue paper is FSC-certified. Hang tags are printed on recycled card stock with soy-based inks. We eliminated single-use plastic polybags from our fulfillment process entirely in early 2025, replacing them with compostable garment bags.

On the shipping side, we consolidate orders wherever possible and partner with carriers that offer carbon-neutral delivery options. We also encourage customers to choose consolidated shipping at checkout, which bundles items into fewer packages.

## What Comes Next

Sustainability is a moving target. We do not claim to be perfect, and we believe that transparency about our progress is more valuable than performative perfection. Here is what we are working toward in the next twelve months:

- **Circularity program:** A take-back initiative where customers can return worn Bazaar garments for recycling or resale
- **Full traceability:** QR codes on every garment linking to a transparency page showing material origins and factory details
- **Carbon footprint labeling:** Clear, product-level carbon impact data on every listing
- **Expanded use of deadstock fabrics:** Incorporating surplus materials from luxury mills into limited-edition collections

## Your Role

Every purchase is a vote for the kind of industry you want to support. By choosing brands that prioritize people and planet alongside product, you send a clear signal that quality and responsibility matter. We are grateful to every Bazaar customer who makes that choice, and we are committed to earning that trust with every collection we release.
